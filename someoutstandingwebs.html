Stanley Kubrick / biography website

I like the way Tubik Studio utilised the bold typography and contrast colours within a limited palette, high readability, and variety of visual effects.

DEMO / festival website

I find very original the way in which the DEMO identity utilises bold typography in a very fluid way; always moving and transforming. The logotype continuously transdorms on screen and the movement is always contained within a set frame, referring to screen parameters.

Romain Granai / personal website

I find very interesting the motion it uses on typography while browsing the web. When we scroll down the webpage and move the mouse, the typography's position, angle, and shape will change simultaneously. Meanwhile, the switch buttons on the website's top can transform the website between positive and negative colours, and make the texts more display.

All websites have been selected from the book 'Typography for Screen: Type in Motion' by Wang Shaoqiang
