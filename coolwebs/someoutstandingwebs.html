All websites have been selected from the book 'Typography for Screen: Type in Motion' by Wang Shaoqiang

STANLEY KUBRICK

BY Tubik Studio

On the July 26th 2018, the date that marked the 90th anniversary of Stanley Kubrick's birthday, tubik Studio presented all the fans of this famous film director, screenwriter, and producer with an elegant biography website devoted to his path of glory life nad creatie heritage in the world cinematography.

I like the way Tubik Studio utilised the bold typography and contrast colours within a limited palette, high readability, and variety of visual effects.

DEMO Festival

BY Studio Dumbar

DEMO founded by Studio Dumbar and Exterion Media Netherlands is a festival celebrating the finest motions from the finest studios, designers, upcoming talents, and art academies from all around the world, showcasing work for 24 hours on all 80 digital screens located in Amsterdam Central Train Station.

I find very original the way in which the DEMO identity utilises bold typography in a very fluid way; always moving and transforming. The logotype continuously transdorms on screen and the movement is always contained within a set frame, referring to screen parameters.

souffl.com

BY Souffl

This is the portfolio website of Souffl. They present their design works, offers, and value proposition.

I find interesting the way they used only one font, Souffl Ultra Condensed which was designed to be responsive. Such a font became the structure of the website graphic design.
